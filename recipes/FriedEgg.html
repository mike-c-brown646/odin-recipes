Fried Egg Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for the most Fried Egg you will every eat.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • green goddess seasoning

Steps

  1. heat up a frying pan
  2. put some olive oil on that pan
  3. crack an egg in the pan without breaking the yoke
  4. season with green goddess and salt
  5. flip egg at desired time
