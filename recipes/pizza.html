Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for the most gorgeous pizza you will every eat. Our pizza sauce has been simmered to perfection for 12 hours using our secret ingredients. This pizza has a soft chewy dough with mozzarella cheese baked a crispy deliciousness. It is topped with gourmet mushrooms, artisan onions and garden fresh green pepper. You will never taste a better pizza.. TRUST.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • mozzarella cheese
  • pizza sauce
  • red onion
  • green pepper
  • mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees on Pizza setting
  2. Press dough out with rolling pin and flour
  3. Place flour on metal pizza peel followed by dough
  4. Add pizza sauce on top of dough
  5. Add cheese on top of sauce
  6. Add toppings on top of cheese
  7. Bake in the oven for 7-9 mins monitoring closely
